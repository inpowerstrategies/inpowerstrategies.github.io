The Program

We know that there are a lot of options when it comes to self-defence courses. Here's a little bit about what you'll learn from ours.

What we're focusing on

The Stay Safe Course covers 4 critical areas.

Falling

This may seem like an odd one, but learning how to fall safely is a skill that is applicable to a range of situations, throughout your lifetime!

Striking

While striking does encompass what you might imagine, well strikes! We also show you how it can help with making space and maintaining your personal space (catchy mnemonics included!).

Blocking

In this segment we cover knowing where the more vulnerable areas are on your body and how to try to best protect them – from standing and on the ground.

Throwing

Our personal favourite, learning a few basic throws is helpful not just as a tool in your toolbox, but also offers alternative ways of dealing with a situation and helps introduce higher levels of physical contact in a more comfortable, fun way.
