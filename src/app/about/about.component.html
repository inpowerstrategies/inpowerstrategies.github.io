About us

InPower Strategies is a self-defence and confidence building initiative for women, by women. The skills learned in these courses will equip you with confidence, physical awareness in a variety of situations, and connect you to a community of like-minded women.

We've pulled and synthesised from a wide range of martial arts and have also incorporated psychology and law to bring a holistic approach to the problem of misogyny & violence against women. We understand that this is a multi-faceted and deeply complex problem.

As a result, our aim is to provide physical self-defence basics while also providing awareness, tips, and references to resources to help psychologically, legally and emotionally. At a broader level, we endeavour to foster a community of women who can share their skills and support one another through the shared activity of taking an InPower Strategies course!


Our Goals

  • Skills to help keep yourself safe – focusing on a reaction-based system and knowing your body.
  • To realise the innate strength within you
  • Walk out of the class with a sense of confidence
  • Fostering a community of encouraging and supportive women for women
  • Knowledge and access to resources to help with the symptoms and repercussions of violence against women
