About us

InPower Inc. is a self-defense and confidence building initiative for women, by women. The skills learned in these courses will equip you with confidence, physical awareness in a variety of situations, and connect you to a community of like-minded women.


Our Goals

  • Skills for defense – rather than choreography, focus on a reaction-based system and knowing your body
  • To realise the innate strength every human body has – to show you are not made of glass
  • Fitness
  • Walk out of the class with a sense of confidence
  • To create a sustainable business model through the program
  • Create a community of encouraging and supportive women for women
